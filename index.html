Academics

Preparing students to make meaningful contributions to society

Business and Professional

Gain real-world job skills in a variety of programs that help build a better community while serving local workforce needs.

Computers & Engineering

Secure the essential skills you need in the growing fields of computer information systems and electrical and computer engineering technologies.

Digital Media

In today’s skilled workforce, there is an increasingly high demand for digital content creation skills. Learn how to design and implement content using the most current technologies for various digital media.

Health

Prepare for a rewarding, high-demand career in a health-related setting, and make a real difference in the lives of others. Train with well-established community partners for real-world, clinical experience.

Technical

Become a highly-skilled professional who is needed now more than ever by local industry in building, manufacturing, engineering, or transportation. Programs of study prepare you for a high-demand, high-tech career in these fields.

Arts & Sciences

An AAS in Interdisciplinary Studies allows students to customize their program based on academic and professional goals.

"At Chattahoochee Tech, you will find a collegiate atmosphere that fosters learning, creative thinking, and personal character development. Ideas are exchanged, friendships are formed, and minds are expanded as our students interact in class and at a wide variety of Campus Life functions. It’s easy to learn and grow here at Chattahoochee Tech."

Ron Newcomb, Ed.D.
